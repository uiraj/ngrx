4) Are you a Qualified Purchaser?

A Qualified Purchaser is

  • 1) Any natural person or entity who owns not less than $5,000,000 in Any company that owns not less than $5,000,000 in investments and that is owned directly or indirectly by two or more natural persons who are related as siblings or spouse, the estates of such persons, or foundations, charitable organizations, or trusts established by of for the benefit of such persons .

  • 2) Any company that owns not less than $5,000,000 in investments and that is owned directly or indirectly by two or more natural persons who are related as siblings or spouse, the estates of such persons, or foundations, charitable organizations, or trusts established by of for the benefit of such persons

  • 3) Any person, acting for its own account or the accounts of other qualified purchasers, who in aggregate owns and invests on a discretionary basis, not less than $25,000,000 in investments
{{qualifiedPurchaser}} Qualified Purchaser type is required!

5) Are you an Accredited Investor?

An Accredited Investor is

  • 1) Any natural person achieving an annual income for two successive years in excess of $200,000, or $300,000 for joint income

  • 2) Any natural person with a net worth exceeding $1,000,000 either individually or jointly

  • 3) Any natural person who is a General Partner, Executive Officer, Director or a related combination thereof for the issuer of unregistered securities

  • 4) Any entity that is a company or an organization with assets exceeding $5,000,000 that is not formed for the sole purpose of purchasing specific unregistered securities.
